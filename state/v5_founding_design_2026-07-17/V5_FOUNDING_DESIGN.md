## 0. DECISION: MOUNT — narrowly, one lever, every exit a measurement

**MOUNT anima-v5** as a single-lever campaign whose first (and possibly only) question is:

> **H5_001 — Is H_005's identification wall a register artifact?** With surface leakage removed
> from the register (and nothing else changed from the sealed v4 configuration), does a learned
> χ̂ = g(φ) on the fixed hand support become non-inferior to the hand field on held-out
> compositional honorific binding?

### Why this is live and not hope

1. **The wall was measured under a register that could not pose the question.** H_005's F1a′
   non-inferiority failed both seeds (margins −0.1758 / −0.2904; arm A-χ̂ vs A-hand,
   `state/h004_parser_duel_tension_rank_drill_2026-07-16/verdict_g3a.json`) — but the G3-0d probe
   read **φ→hon = 1.0 held-out, n=1200** (arm: frozen-trunk logistic probe,
   `HYPOTHESES/cards/H_005_learned_chi_values.md:73`, honest-limit (a) scoped on the frozen
   record). With the concord bit encoded near-verbatim from surface, the drill loss admits every
   χ̂ that fits the leaky drill; the hand field is one point in a large solution set and the loss
   carries no pressure to select it. The autonomy-of-values question was answered only in its
   leaky variant. K3 stands as measured; what it MEANS is capped, and the cap is on the record.
2. **The learned field is misidentified, not inert.** F1a itself PASSED both seeds:
   Δ(A-χ̂ − C-χ̂plc) = 0.2982 / 0.1914 ≥ 0.15 (same `verdict_g3a.json`) — what the learned values
   carry still requires correct edge alignment. A dead mechanism would not clear its placebo.
3. **v4 measured both failure modes of a learnable register — in separate experiments — and never
   measured whether daylight exists between them.** The leak side: H_005's honest-limit (probe
   1.0, templatic surface encodes the bit verbatim). The unlearnable side: H_008's A7 defect —
   enforcing heuristic-chance via silent syllable pairing made class a 2-syllable parity
   conjunction, SGD-hard, control at in-sample CHANCE (0.5) at every budget while 16/16 audits
   were green (`HYPOTHESES/cards/H_008_supervision_budget_sample_efficiency.md`, A7′ fix). A
   deleaked register lives exactly between these two modes: local surface uninformative, the
   structural conjunction still learnable. **Whether a window exists between them on this
   substrate is an empirical question v4 never asked, and it is answerable for ~$0 (gate G-0k
   below).** If the window does not exist, v5 folds at near-$0 and THAT measurement is the honest
   DON'T-MOUNT record: autonomy is unposable at 3.7M/byte because leak and unlearnability touch.
4. **Cost honesty**: the entire H5_001 ladder is local-MPS at v4 scale (CPT+probe was "tens of
   minutes, $0 cash" per the G3-0d recipe, H_005 card:71; full arms trained overnight locally in
   H_004/H_007). First verdict < 1 week of box time, $0 cloud. No QFORGE spend before a measured
   positive opens the conditional second gate (§1, scale).

**DON'T-MOUNT was weighed and rejected** — but conditionally, and the condition is priced: v5's
first gate (G-0k) IS the mount/fold referee. If no register-hardness window exists, v5 records
DON'T-CONTINUE with the measurement in hand, and the remaining doors (≥8M/jamo substrate) stay
shut until someone produces admissibility arithmetic for them (none exists today — see §1).

### Ranking the three recorded reopen doors

**1st — (ii) non-templatic register (probe < 1.0): OPEN NOW.** It attacks the identification wall
directly, plausibly dissolves the saturation wall, costs ~nothing to referee, and changes exactly
one variable — attribution survives.

**2nd — (i) ≥8M params and/or jamo/word tokenization: CONDITIONAL, NOT NOW.** Named disagreement
with the recorded reopen conditions (`CAMPAIGN_RESULT.md` §4 lists (i) first, co-equal): the only
direct capacity probe v4 ran says scale does NOT bind on the values question — "a bigger module
worsens it" (the s0 drill-1.0 / f2″-0.8242 gap, CAMPAIGN_RESULT.md §3, H_005 K3). The ≥8M
literature anchor (0.46–0.50, directed attachment) belongs to the SUPPORT question, which is
sequenced behind values. Scale-first would spend more to re-ask a question still confounded by
leakage. Scale opens ONLY if (a) H5_001 passes and the re-derived support arithmetic still shows
negative headroom at 3.7M — then ≥8M/jamo is the priced next move with the anchor finally
applicable — or (b) never on the values question without new arithmetic, because the measured
evidence points the other way. Tokenization note for that day: jamo preserves the free-slot metric
and swap-alignment trick (fixed inventory, deterministic decomposition — a port, not a loss);
word-level kills both and should be presumed inadmissible for this panel family.

**3rd — (iii) external pretrained parser: REJECTED as premise.** mech-1's sealed claim already
covers delivered fields — F1 Δd_acc(A-duel − A-rank1) = 0.3789/0.3802, A-duel f2″ 1.0
(`state/h004_.../train_result_full.json` + `verdict.json`). A pretrained parser DELIVERS the
field; its success re-proves a sealed claim with a fancier deliverer and says nothing about
autonomy. Also rejected as an audit oracle for natural text: parser error would enter the truth
conditions, breaking the closed-form audit discipline. If a parser ever enters, it is a new
campaign with a new thesis, not anima-v5.

A successor changing all three at once could attribute its result to nothing. v5 changes one.

## 2. The register: constructed non-templatic (the crux of the design)

**Not natural text.** "Non-templatic" is operationalized as a MEASURED property of a register we
still generate ourselves, so every G-0 audit stays closed-form (H_008's whole G-0 discipline
assumed a constructed grammar — v5 keeps that assumption true):

- Same Korean honorific-concord semantics family as H_004 (the sealed positive's operation).
- Surface variation by construction: K paraphrase frames per proposition; licensed word-order
  alternation; optional adjuncts; **distractor honorific morphemes** (시/님/께서-class material in
  positions that do NOT participate in the measured concord); length/position jitter so the
  concord bit has no fixed surface locus. Hardness dial **δ** = (frame count, distractor rate,
  jitter width).
- Because we generate it: truth conditions are computed at generation time (exact), surface
  leakage is measurable by **exhaustive n-gram census over the finite generated corpus**
  (closed-form, not sampled), and lexeme-construction constraints are verifiable by direct count.

The deleaked register must sit between two measured failure modes:
- **Leak side (H_005 honest-limit)**: local surface features predict the bit ⇒ probe ≈ 1.0 via
  verbatim encoding ⇒ drill loss carries no identifying pressure.
- **Parity side (H_008 A7)**: the bit recoverable ONLY through a high-order conjunction ⇒
  SGD-hard ⇒ control at in-sample chance while every balance audit is green.

The design consequence: the frozen-trunk probe bar **flips meaning instead of flipping sign**.
G3-0d required probe ≥ 0.90 as a build gate (χ̂ must be able to read concord — H_005 card:66-73);
v5 KEEPS that bar, and adds the corpus-level census at chance. With the census green, a high probe
certifies STRUCTURAL recovery (the trunk composed distributed evidence), not verbatim copying —
the honest-limit defect is cured at the source, and the probe becomes confirmation instead of
confound. The reopen condition's literal wording ("probe reads < 1.0") is satisfied as a
by-product (verbatim reading is impossible when no short surface feature carries the bit), but the
enforced invariant is census-at-chance + probe-above-floor, which is the defect-encoding form.

## 3. H5_001 — pre-register seed

**Hypothesis.** On the sealed v4 trunk configuration with a constructed deleaked register at
hardness δ* (chosen by G-0k, frozen before G-1), a learned χ̂ = g(φ) (~3k bilinear, fixed hand
support — H_005's exact module family) is non-inferior (margin m = 0.10) to the hand-staged field
on the held-out multi-bind free-slot panel, both seeds.

**One variable.** Register (templatic → deleaked-at-δ*). Trunk, tokenization, support, χ̂ family,
panel machinery, seed count: all held at the H_004/H_005 sealed configuration.

**Panel.** f2″-analog multi-bind free-slot panel REGENERATED in the new register: codebook
re-derived, GF(2) rank audit re-run, free-slot set recomputed, field-blind ceiling recomputed
(0.667 is a v4 number that died with its codebook — never inherited).

**Why heuristics sit at chance — audits, each naming the defect it encodes:**
- **A1 surface census** (encodes H_005 honest-limit (a)): max over all surface n-grams (n ≤ 4,
  byte-level) of concord-bit predictiveness ≤ 0.5 + ε (ε pre-registered from corpus-size
  arithmetic at G-0, placeholder 0.05) by EXACT COUNT over the generated corpus.
- **A2 learnability** (encodes H_008's A7 parity defect): trained control fits IN-SAMPLE ≥ 0.95
  at δ*, AND frozen-trunk probe φ→hon ≥ 0.90 held-out (the retained G3-0d build gate).
  Heuristic-chance is certified ONLY jointly with learnability, never alone.
- **A3 lexeme-construction** (encodes the silent-pairing defect): no cross-lexeme syllable
  sharing, non-corpus pools — verified by direct census, A7′ carried verbatim.
- **A4 balance/liveness** set re-derived per H_005 G3-0 (never inherited).

**Falsifiers (the ≥5, with the two inherited non-negotiables):**
- **F1a′-analog** (the verdict falsifier): A-χ̂ f2″ ≥ A-hand f2″ − 0.10, both seeds.
  *Admissibility arithmetic against a TRAINED-control anchor, measured in G-1 BEFORE the learned
  arm exists*: (reachability) A-hand − C-trained ≥ 2m = 0.20; (trained-control ceiling)
  C-trained f2″ ≤ 1 − 2m = 0.80; (comparator floor) A-hand ≥ 0.90; all on THIS panel at THIS δ*,
  both seeds. If any fails, H5_001 is INADMISSIBLE-not-falsified and the pre-registered single
  retreat (below) applies.
- **F1a placebo**: Δ(A-χ̂ − C-χ̂plc) ≥ 0.15 — passed in H_005 (0.2982/0.1914); regression = kill.
- **L1 eff-rank** (inherited non-negotiable): Δd_acc(A-χ̂ − A-χ̂-rank1) ≥ 0.15 — the learned field
  must beat its own rank-1 compression, else it is v1's emit-controller in new clothes.
- **L2 ablate-the-channel** (inherited non-negotiable): strip-resolution on A-χ̂ collapses —
  d_dacc(A-χ̂ strip) ≥ 0.5 × d_dacc(A-hand strip, measured at G-1). Bar is a FORMULA off the G-1
  comparator, not H_004's templatic 1.0→0.5 inherited as a constant. (H_005 measured
  F5_d_dacc 0.3242/0.2096 — a fixed 0.25 bar would have been half-inadmissible; the formula form
  is the fix.) Erasure method: INLP-style iterative, as upgraded in H_008's design (single rank-2
  projection measured Δ = 0.0 — `CHANGELOG.md` 2026-07-17 entry).
- **F2 fit-integrity** (encodes H_005's instability defect as a named falsifier, not a wiring
  footnote): drill d_acc(A-χ̂) ≥ 0.95 BOTH seeds AND cross-seed drill spread ≤ 0.05. One refit
  per arm-seed with a fresh init allowed, logged; a second miss = K-fit close. (H_005 s1 drill
  0.832 tripped exactly this as "WIRING FAILURE" — `verdict_g3a.json` top line.)
- **Panel-integrity set** (carried): F3 C-scaf < 0.60 · F4 off-top rank-mass ≥ 0.20 ·
  F6 placebo-gap + C-perm ∈ [0.45, 0.55], all recomputed on the new panel.

**Gate ladder (cheapest first):**
- **G-0** ($0, hours): grammar + generator + closed-form audits A1/A3/A4 + codebook GF(2) audit +
  ε arithmetic. No training.
- **G-0k — window-existence pre-check** (the H_008 cliff lesson transplanted; ~5h MPS, $0):
  sweep δ over ≥5 spaced settings; per δ: A1 census (closed-form) + one CPT per seed + probe +
  control in-sample fit (the tens-of-minutes G3-0d recipe). **Require ≥2 consecutive δ with
  census ≤ 0.5+ε AND in-sample ≥ 0.95 AND probe ≥ 0.90** — a window, not a knife-edge. The δ*
  frozen = the easier of the two in-window settings. If the leak→unlearnable transition is a
  CLIFF (no two consecutive settings), **H5_001 closes K1 at ~$0 and v5 FOLDS** — the measured
  DON'T-CONTINUE.
- **G-1 — comparator re-seal + trained-control ceiling** (~2 days MPS): A-hand, C-trained
  (compute-matched), C-scaf at δ*, 2 seeds, full panel. This is controls-first at target scale
  (standing G-1.5 gate) AND the transfer test of mech-1's sealed claim into the new register.
  Admissibility arithmetic above evaluated here. ONE pre-registered retreat allowed (to the other
  in-window δ from G-0k) if the control saturates or the hand arm fails; a second miss = fold
  (no knee-fishing — H_007's rescue analysis flagged exactly this fragility; H_008 capped pre-run
  drills at 8 for the same reason).
- **G-2 — the learned arm** (~2 days MPS): A-χ̂ 2 seeds + A-χ̂-rank1 + strip + placebo + full
  falsifier set. Verdict collector frozen and unit-tested BEFORE the run (H_008's
  collector-finalized pattern, commit e1b2a00).

## 4. Cost + kill

G-1 ~2 days · G-2 ~2 days. Peak memory = v4's d=384 footprint (already proven on this box).

plus re-derived support arithmetic (§1).

**Kill ledger (every exit a measurement):**
- **K-fold-1 (G-0k, ~$0)**: no window between leak and unlearnability ⇒ v5 folds. Record: the
  deleaked-register lever does not exist at 3.7M/byte; remaining doors (≥8M/jamo) stay shut
  pending admissibility arithmetic nobody currently has.
- **K-fold-2 (G-1)**: hand field fails in the deleaked register both seeds ⇒ mech-1's placement
  claim is register-bound ⇒ v5 folds with the sealed claim SHARPENED (a real result: the field
  format requires the templatic surface). Control saturation after the one retreat ⇒ fold as
  inadmissible-here.
- **K-fold-3 (G-2)**: F1a′-analog fails both seeds with F2 fit-integrity GREEN and A1/A2 in-band
  ⇒ autonomy-of-values is falsified under the one condition H_005 could not test. Per the
  values→support ordering, the entire learned-tension program at 3.7M closes. **Pre-commitment:
  no third register, no harder-δ fishing, no scale escalation on the values question** — the ≥8M
  door then requires a literature anchor for values-identification at scale (the analog of
  H_006's 0.46–0.50 support anchor) before any spend.
- **PASS**: the learned-values half of autonomy is open at 3.7M ⇒ next gate is re-deriving the
  E[A-tduel] = a + b·r coefficients on the new panel to price whether SUPPORT (H_006's question)
  is admissible here or needs the scale door.

## 5. Lesson-enforcement map (where each inherited gate lives in this design)

1. **Admissibility has two halves** (reachability + `verification.trained-control-ceiling`) →
   G-1 is structurally controls-first at target scale/register; F1a′ arithmetic demands
   A-hand − C-trained ≥ 2m AND C-trained ≤ 1 − 2m; every anchor measured on THIS panel at THIS
   δ*, both seeds; nothing inherited from the templatic register.
2. **`audit-wrong-property-parity-1`** (audits must encode the defect) → A1 encodes the verbatim
   leak at its source (corpus census, not the probe symptom); A2 encodes the parity trap
   (learnability co-certified with heuristic-chance — the exact pairing whose absence let 16/16
   green audits certify an unlearnable panel); A3 carries A7′ verbatim; G-0k measures
   learnability at every candidate δ instead of assuming it.
3. **Metric-ceiling defects** → codebook re-derived + GF(2) rank audit + free-slot set recomputed
   + field-blind ceiling recomputed, all in G-0, none inherited (0.667 dies with v4).
4. **d_acc discipline + cite-with-arm** → collector frozen and unit-tested before the verdict run
   (H_008 pattern); schema requires arm + source path per number; f1/f2 remain PANEL names;
   d_acc bounded 1.0, chance 0.5.
5. **NEW, from H_008's close (the priced cliff lesson)** → G-0k window-existence pre-check:
   ≥2 consecutive in-window settings required before anything trains — the knee-not-cliff guard
   applied to the register dial, which is the only band-like object in H5_001.

  probe's verbatim reading disappears as a consequence. §2.
